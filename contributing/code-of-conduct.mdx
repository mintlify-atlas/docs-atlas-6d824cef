The Worlds Platform is committed to fostering a welcoming, professional, and inclusive community. Everyone who participates in this project — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to adhere to these standards.

## Our standards

Behaviors that contribute to a positive community environment include:

- Treating all participants with respect and dignity.
- Focusing on constructive, technical feedback.
- Welcoming contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.
- Being direct and clear without being dismissive or condescending.
- Acknowledging and learning from mistakes gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in any project space:

- Harassment, intimidation, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Discriminatory language or imagery based on gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, disability, national origin, or any other protected characteristic.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information about others without explicit consent.
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers immediately. You can open a confidential report by emailing [support@wazoo.dev](mailto:support@wazoo.dev).

All reports will be reviewed promptly and treated with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
- Temporarily or permanently suspend community members for repeated or serious violations.

Enforcement decisions are made at the discretion of the maintainers and reflect the goal of maintaining a healthy, productive community for everyone.

<Note>
  This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces: GitHub Issues, pull requests, code reviews, the Discord community, and any other official channel.
</Note>
